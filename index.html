About Us

Empowering Communities Since 2016

The AGE TRUST, established in 2016 under the Indian Trusts Act, is a registered profit non-organization committed to empowering disadvantaged communities. Our mission is to foster an equitable society by enhancing access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. Through skill development, micro finance, women's empowerment, livelihood generation, environmental conservation, and community development, we aim to uplift marginalised individuals and families, creating a sustainable and inclusive future for all.

Vision

To empower weaker sections of society, uplift low-income families and communities, and create equity by providing access to education, healthcare, and women's empowerment opportunities.

Mission

  • Quality Education: Ensuring every child has the opportunity to learn and grow.
  • Healthcare: Delivering quality healthcare services to underserved communities.
  • Economic Empowerment: Promoting sustainable livelihoods through skill development and micro finance.
  • Women's Empowerment: Providing education, training, and financial support to women.
  • Environmental Conservation: Advocating sustainable practices and protecting natural resources.
  • Community Strengthening: Building resilient communities through infrastructure development and social initiatives.

Core Values

1. Empowerment and Inclusion

Community-driven development that amplifies marginalized voices and equips individuals to shape their futures.

2. Sustainability and Innovation

A holistic approach to addressing social, economic, and environmental challenges while embracing innovation for long-term resilience.

3. Transparency and Accountability

Ethical practices, open communication, and measurable impact to build trust and accountability within communities.
