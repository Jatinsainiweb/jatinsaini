About Us

Empowering Communities Since 2016

AGE Trust, established under the Indian Trusts Act, is a beacon of hope for marginalized communities. We are committed to driving social change through our focus on healthcare, education, skill development, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

To empower communities for sustainable development through better healthcare, education, skills, and environmental care.

Our Mission

  • Providing Access to Quality Education
  • Improving Healthcare
  • Promoting Economic Empowerment
  • Empowering Women
  • Protecting the Environment
  • Strengthening Communities
Our Impact

What We Do

Over the past years, AGE Trust has made significant strides in improving the lives of marginalized communities through impactful initiatives.

Health Camps

Providing essential healthcare services and raising awareness on sanitation, maternal and child health, and disease prevention.

Street Children Program

Offering counseling and educational support to guide children toward safer, healthier lives.

Self-Help Groups

Empowering women and families through financial literacy, enabling them to save, access loans, and start small businesses.

Skill Development

Offering vocational training to enhance employability and create income-generating opportunities.
